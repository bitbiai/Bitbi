3. Data Collection on This Website

Server Log Files

The hosting provider automatically collects and stores information in server log files (e.g., browser type/version, operating system, referrer URL, hostname, time of request, IP address).

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR (legitimate interest in the technically error-free and secure operation of this website).

Contact Form

When you send an inquiry via the contact form, your data (name, email, message) is processed to handle your request.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR (pre-contractual/contract-related communication) or Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR (legitimate interest in responding to general inquiries).

4. Hosting

This website is hosted by an external provider (Cloudflare). Personal data processed on this website may be stored and processed on the hoster's servers.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR (legitimate interest in secure and efficient provision of the website) and, where applicable, Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR (where hosting is required to provide requested services).

5. SSL / TLS Encryption

This site uses SSL/TLS encryption to protect transmitted data.

6. Consent Management – Cookiebot

This website uses Cookiebot (Usercentrics A/S, Denmark) to manage consents for cookies and similar technologies. Cookiebot stores your selection using a technically necessary cookie.

You can change or revoke your consent at any time via the cookie banner or the footer link ("Cookie settings").

More info: cookiebot.com/en/privacy-policy

7. Third-Party Services & Performance Tools

YouTube Embeds

YouTube videos are only loaded after explicit consent via Cookiebot. When activated, a connection to YouTube/Google is established and personal data (e.g., IP address and technical request data) may be transmitted; cookies or similar identifiers may also be stored on your device.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (a) GDPR (consent).

More info: policies.google.com/privacy

Tailwind CSS (CDN)

We load Tailwind CSS from a CDN. This may transmit your IP address and technical request data to the CDN provider.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (a) GDPR (consent), if activated via Cookiebot.

CoinGecko API

We use the CoinGecko API to display live crypto data. When retrieving this data, your IP address and technical request data may be transmitted to CoinGecko.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR (legitimate interest in providing up-to-date information).

More info: coingecko.com/en/privacy

Cloudflare Real User Measurements (RUM)

This website uses Cloudflare Real User Measurements (RUM) to measure and improve real-world performance (e.g., load times, Core Web Vitals, error rates).

Provider: Cloudflare, Inc. (USA). When activated, a JavaScript snippet collects performance and technical usage data (e.g., page URL, referrer where transmitted, timestamp, device/browser information, performance metrics). Cloudflare may also process network data (including IP address) for providing the service and for security purposes.

Purpose: performance optimization and error analysis.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (a) GDPR (consent), if activated via Cookiebot.

You can withdraw your consent at any time via the Cookiebot settings (footer link).

More info: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy

8. Cookies

We set no own first-party cookies except the technically necessary Cookiebot consent cookie. Third-party services (e.g., YouTube, CDN resources, performance measurements) are only activated after your consent.
