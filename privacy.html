How we handle your data on this website

3. Data Collection on This Website

Server Log Files

The hosting provider automatically collects and stores information in server log files (e.g., browser type/version, operating system, referrer URL, hostname, time of request, IP address).

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR (legitimate interest in the technically error-free and secure operation of this website).

Contact Form

When you send an inquiry via the contact form, your data (name, email, message) is processed to handle your request.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR (pre-contractual/contract-related communication) or Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR (legitimate interest in responding to general inquiries).

4. Hosting

This website is hosted by an external provider (Cloudflare). Personal data processed on this website may be stored and processed on the hoster's servers.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR (legitimate interest in secure and efficient provision of the website) and, where applicable, Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR (where hosting is required to provide requested services).

5. SSL / TLS Encryption

This site uses SSL/TLS encryption to protect transmitted data.

6. Consent Management

This website uses a custom cookie consent banner to manage your preferences for cookies and similar technologies. Your consent choice is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and no data is transmitted to third parties for consent management.

The banner is displayed to visitors in the European Economic Area (EEA) upon first visit. You can change or revoke your consent at any time via the footer link ("Cookie Settings").

Categories: Necessary (always active, consent storage), Analytics (performance measurement), Marketing (embedded content such as YouTube videos).

7. Third-Party Services & Performance Tools

YouTube Embeds

YouTube videos are embedded using the privacy-enhanced mode (youtube-nocookie.com). When loaded, a connection to YouTube/Google is established and personal data (e.g., IP address and technical request data) may be transmitted; cookies or similar identifiers may also be stored on your device.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (a) GDPR (consent).

More info: policies.google.com/privacy

Google Fonts (CDN)

We load display fonts from Google Fonts CDN. This may transmit your IP address and technical request data to Google.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR (legitimate interest in website presentation). No cookies are set by loading Google Fonts.

CoinGecko API

We use the CoinGecko API to display live crypto data. When retrieving this data, your IP address and technical request data may be transmitted to CoinGecko.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR (legitimate interest in providing up-to-date information).

More info: coingecko.com/en/privacy

Cloudflare Real User Measurements (RUM)

This website uses Cloudflare Real User Measurements (RUM) to measure and improve real-world performance (e.g., load times, Core Web Vitals, error rates).

Provider: Cloudflare, Inc. (USA). When activated, a JavaScript snippet collects performance and technical usage data (e.g., page URL, referrer where transmitted, timestamp, device/browser information, performance metrics). Cloudflare may also process network data (including IP address) for providing the service and for security purposes.

Purpose: performance optimization and error analysis.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (a) GDPR (consent).

You can withdraw your consent at any time via the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer.

More info: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy

8. Cookies

We set no own first-party cookies. Consent preferences are stored in your browser's localStorage (not as a cookie). Third-party services that may set cookies (e.g., YouTube, performance measurements) are disclosed above. You can manage your preferences at any time via the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer.

9. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and data portability. To exercise these rights, contact us at bit@bitbi.ai.

Right to lodge a complaint (Art. 77 GDPR): You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, in particular in the Member State of your habitual residence, place of work, or the place of the alleged infringement.

10. Data Retention

We delete personal data as soon as the purpose no longer applies or legal retention periods expire.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Please check this page regularly.
